Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 29, 2026

ClassicTube ("we," "our," or "the Extension") is committed to protecting user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage for the ClassicTube browser extension.

1. No Data Collection

ClassicTube does not collect, store, record, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or user data. We do not have access to your Google or YouTube account credentials.

2. Technical Operation

The Extension operates entirely locally on your browser. Its sole function is to apply custom CSS (Cascading Style Sheets) and local scripts to modify the visual interface of YouTube. No data is sent to external servers or third parties.

3. Permissions Justification

  • Host Permissions (youtube.com): Required strictly to inject visual styles and restore the legacy aesthetic of the YouTube interface.
  • Tabs: Used only to provide an onboarding experience by opening our official website (classictube.io) upon initial installation.

4. Data Security

Since we do not collect any data, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information through our Extension.

5. Intellectual Property

All rights, titles, and interests in and to ClassicTube, including its original code and design elements, are exclusively owned by the ClassicTube Team.

6. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Continued use of the Extension constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

If you have any questions regarding this policy, please contact us via our official repository at GitHub.
