Privacy Policy

ClassicTube is built with a "Privacy First" philosophy. We believe that your digital focus should not come at the cost of your personal data.

1. Data Collection

ClassicTube does not collect, store, or share any personal user data. The extension operates entirely locally within your browser. We have no servers tracking your history, search queries, or habits.

2. Data Security

No data is transmitted to external servers. All modifications to the YouTube interface happen locally on your device. All rights and intellectual property belong to the ClassicTube Team.

Our website may contain links to the Chrome Web Store or support platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.
